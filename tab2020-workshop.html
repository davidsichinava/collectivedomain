Bridging the Researcher-Practitioner Divide

Tbilisi Architecture Biennial 2020

Build It and They Will Come

A workshop and exhibition at the Tbilisi Architecture Biennial 2020 examining how design researchers can bridge the gap between academic knowledge production and real-world practice.

The project — titled "Build It and They Will Come: Bridging the Researcher-Practitioner Divide" — explored methods for connecting urban research with design practice, policy-making, and community engagement. The workshop brought together researchers, practitioners, architects, and activists to develop collaborative frameworks for socially engaged spatial research.

A publication documenting the workshop and its findings was produced as part of the Tbilisi Architecture Biennial 2020 Catalogue.
