Project Overview

Large-Scale Urban Development and Social Impact

Cities in Eurasia have experienced overwhelming urban transformations through building demolitions, renovations, and new construction.

Our research focuses on two scales of urban development: large iconic flagship projects (over 75,000 m²) and medium-large residential infill projects. We examine their social impacts and their relationship to gentrification, displacement, and spatial inequality.

This project is supported by the SSHRC Insight Development Grant (2023–25) and the National Science Foundation of Georgia Grant (2018–22), conducted in partnership with Tbilisi State University and the Socio-Spatial Georgia network.
