Infographics

As a part of our research into the erosion of public space in Tbilisi, Georgia, we created the following infographics.

Many people are unaware of the current threats facing public space in Tbilisi. These Creative Commons (CC) infographics are aimed at highlighting the city's specific threats. Please feel free to download, print, and circulate them widely under the terms of the Creative Commons license.
