Our Team

Partners

Suzanne Harris-Brandts

Partner | HBASc, M.Arch, PhD, OAA, LEED AP BD+C

harrisbrandts@kolektiuri.com

Suzanne Harris-Brandts is a licensed architect (OAA) and certified LEED AP BD+C, and co-founder of Collective Domain. She is an Assistant Professor of Architecture & Urbanism and a Faculty Associate at the Institute of European, Russian & Eurasian Studies at Carleton University.

Suzanne holds a PhD in Urban and Regional Studies from MIT and a Master of Architecture from the University of Waterloo. Her research focuses on the intersection of architecture, politics, and urban development in post-socialist Eurasia, with particular attention to heritage, displacement, and identity.

David Gogishvili

Partner | BA, MGeog, MUP, PhD

gogishvili@kolektiuri.com

David Gogishvili is co-founder of Collective Domain and a SNF-Senior-Researcher at the University of Lausanne's Institute of Geography and Sustainability. He was previously a postdoctoral researcher at Malmö University, Sweden.

David holds a doctoral degree in Urban Studies and Regional Science from the Gran Sasso Science Institute (2017) and Master's degrees in Human Geography (Tbilisi State University) and Urban and Regional Planning (Stockholm University). He has been actively involved in NGOs and urban social movements, and is the founder of the Soviet Past Research Laboratory and Iare Pekhit (Walk).

David Sichinava

Partner | BA, MGeog, PhD

sichinava@kolektiuri.com

David Sichinava is co-founder of Collective Domain and an Adjunct Research Professor at Carleton University's Institute of European, Eurasian and Russian Studies. His research spans political geography, electoral studies, and urban social movements in the South Caucasus and post-Soviet space.

He has published extensively on Georgian politics, electoral geography, urban activism, and the social impacts of urban development in Tbilisi and the wider South Caucasus region.
