Project Overview

Mega-Events and Urban Transformation

Since the 1960s, large-scale events have risen internationally as agents of urban change.

Our research examines how mega-events — including the Olympics, FIFA World Cup, Formula 1 Grand Prix, Commonwealth Games, and European Youth Olympic Festival — reshape cities, governance, and public space. We focus particularly on post-socialist and Global East contexts, where mega-events often coincide with accelerated urban transformation and the deployment of exceptional governance measures.

Key case studies include the Baku Formula 1 City Circuit (Azerbaijan), the 2014 Glasgow Commonwealth Games (Scotland), the European Youth Olympic Festival in the South Caucasus, and the Tbilisi Architecture Biennial.
